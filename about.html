About Solara

Our journey to revolutionize sun protection for Indian skin

Our Story

Solara was born from a simple observation: Indian consumers deserve sunscreen that actually works for their skin, their climate, and their lifestyle.

Founded in 2022 by dermatologists and skincare experts, we noticed a significant gap in the market. Most sunscreens were either formulated for Western skin tones (leaving an undesirable white cast) or failed to perform in India's diverse climate conditions.

Our team spent over two years in research and development, working with leading dermatologists, cosmetic chemists, and real users across India to create formulations that address the unique challenges faced by Indian skin.

Today, Solara stands as India's first premium-mass sunscreen brand that combines science-backed formulations with affordability, making premium sun protection accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To provide every Indian with access to premium, effective sun protection that understands their unique skin needs and climate challenges, making sun safety a daily habit rather than a chore.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted sunscreen brand by continuously innovating and creating products that solve real skin problems while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.
